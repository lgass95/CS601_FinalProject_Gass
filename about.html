About Me

Bio

I am originally from Reading, MA and spent my undergrad years studying in Washington, D.C. After graduating and spending some time working in the non-profit sector, I have returned to the Boston area to continue my studies. My main interests in web development revolve around front-end engineering with HTML and CSS as well as working with databases using languages like SQL. One of my goals is to develop a skillset that will equip me to provide website-building services to small businesses.

When I am not studying web development, I spend my time exercising, studying personal training and nutrition, and exploring my neighborhood on foot. One of my favorite things to do when out and about is discover new restaurants and coffee shops. I enjoy travel and have been to several parts of the United States, as well as to Turkey and England. I hope to one day take separate trips to Japan and across Europe.
